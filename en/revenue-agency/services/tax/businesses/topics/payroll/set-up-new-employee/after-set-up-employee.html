After you set up your employee

Get ready to make deductions

You need to calculate CPP contributions, EI premiums and the amount of federal, provincial (except Quebec) and territorial income tax that you have to deduct from amounts you pay each pay period.

A pay period means the period for which you pay earnings or other remuneration to an employee.
