Supporting the claim on line 45700 of the tax return

To support the claim for the employee and partner GST/HST rebate, send us only the information we are asking for that applies to your client's situation.

Send us a completed Form GST370, Employee and Partner GST/HST Rebate Application, and a list of all qualifying expenses. Make sure to include the employer or partnership business number.

If your client is an employee, also send us:

  • a breakdown of the expenses your client claimed that were subject to GST/HST
  • Form T2200, Declaration of Employment Conditions, completed and signed by your client's employer

If your client is a partner, also send us a copy of the partnership's income or loss statement showing your client's share of the income or loss and a statement of the expenses that your client deducted from their share of the partnership income

Note

Self-employed persons who are not members of a registered partnership cannot claim the rebate on their income tax return.

We need a letter from your client's employer confirming whether the employer is a listed financial institution referred to in paragraph 149(1)(a) of the Excise Tax Act, such as:

  • banks
  • investment dealers
  • trust companies
  • insurance companies
  • credit unions
  • corporations whose principal business is the lending of money
