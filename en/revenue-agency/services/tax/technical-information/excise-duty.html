The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) publishes a broad selection of technical publications and other helpful information relating to excise duties on beer under the Excise Act and on cannabis products, spirits, tobacco products, vaping products, and wine under the Excise Act, 2001.

Publications and information

Rates

Rates of excise duty imposed on beer, spirits, wine, and cannabis, tobacco and vaping products

Vaping products

Apply for a vaping product licence or as an importer of vaping products, register for the stamping regime, complete a return

Cannabis products

Apply for a cannabis licence, register for the stamping regime, complete a cannabis return

Forms

Forms relating to approved formulations, denatured or specially denatured alcohol, beer, spirits, wine, and cannabis, tobacco, and vaping products

Memoranda

Detailed technical information about excise duties on spirits, wine, and cannabis, tobacco, and vaping products

Notices

Announcements about legislation and policies relating to excise duties on beer, spirits, wine, and cannabis, tobacco, and vaping products

Circulars

Detailed technical information about excise duties on beer

Tables of alcoholometry

Tables used to determine the volume and absolute ethyl alcohol content of alcohol

Stamps

Description, purpose, what to look for on products you buy
